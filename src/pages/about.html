About

My name is Paul Le Henaff. I love the CG arts and spend most of my time working on videos game assets, still renders and animations.

I have used Blender for a number of years and am very comfortable with it. Today it's my software of choice for modeling. In the past I have used XSI and 3DS Max but switched to Blender because at the time it could do a lot more than the others e.g. Sculpting, and it has a great community (and it's free!).

Currently my main focus is making Physically Based Rendering (PBR) ready assets for UE4.

I have a number of years of experience working as part of indie teams. I started when I was 12, learning about mods in Half Life 2 and Grand Theft Auto 3. I quickly learned Photoshop and XSI Mod tool to create new assets and added them to the game.

Then, I learned about XNA Game Studio. It was an SDK which allowed developers to create and publish games through the XBox Live Arcade marketplace. I joined a small indie team as a 2D artist and created all the sprite assets and animations for sidescroller platformer and space shooter games.

These days, I'm the Art Lead at Druid Gameworks. We are working on an open world RPG built in UE4 called Witanlore.
